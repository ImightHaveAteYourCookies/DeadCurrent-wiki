# Progression Guide

---

## Early Game: Survive the Dark

### Goals
- Secure shelter immediately
- Avoid deep caves
- Begin mechanical infrastructure

### Focus
- Basic rotational power
- Starter ore processing
- Defensive perimeter

---

## Mid Game: Industrialization

### Goals
- Automate mining
- Expand power production
- Upgrade armor and tools

### Focus
- Mechanical drills
- Digital mining systems
- Automated smelting and ore multiplication

Manual mining should now be minimal.

---

## Late Game: Preparation

### Goals
- Fully automate resource production
- Establish high-tier energy systems
- Prepare for the final boss encounter

Infrastructure becomes the priority over exploration.
