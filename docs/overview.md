# Overview

DeadCurrent transforms Minecraft into a strategic industrial survival experience.

## World Difficulty

- Increased mob health
- Increased mob damage
- Higher cave spawn rates
- Extremely dangerous nighttime overworld

Early-game exploration is intentionally punishing. Survival depends on preparation and automation rather than combat grinding.

## Core Objective

Progress through industrial technology to defeat the Wither Storm and permanently reduce global mob difficulty.

# Lore Overview:

### The Incident
It began at the **Aethel-Gard Research Facility**. A breakthrough in "Alchemical Fusion" went catastrophically wrong, leading to a global atmospheric leak. This wasn't just a gas; it was a mutagenic catalyst that rewrote the laws of biology and physics.

### The Evolution
The "Leak" didn't just kill—it forced a billion years of evolution into a single month. Flora became predatory, and the undead gained a hive-mind intelligence. Human military technology, reliant on traditional chemistry, was neutralized as the atmosphere itself became corrosive to basic polymers and gunpowder.

### The Witherstorm Paradox
In a desperate bid to "clean" the atmosphere, the remaining scientists released a modified Wither construct designed to consume the chemical pollutants. It worked too well. It didn't just consume the chemicals; it integrated them. 

The Witherstorm is now a living embodiment of the Leak. However, because it has processed the entire chemical spectrum of the catastrophe, its **Command Block core** contains the only stabilized antibodies (The Cure) that can restore the world.

### The Path Forward
Players must utilize **Create-tier engineering** and advanced weaponry to pierce the Storm's reinforced hide. You are not just surviving; you are reclaiming a planet.
