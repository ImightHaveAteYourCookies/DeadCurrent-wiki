Overview

DeadCurrent is a high-difficulty survival modpack built for Minecraft 1.20.1 using Forge. The core experience centers around extreme mob danger — caves and nighttime exploration are intentionally lethal. Direct combat and manual mining are heavily discouraged in favor of automation, machinery, and strategic progression.

Survival depends on leveraging technology from major mods like Create and Mekanism to reduce player exposure to hostile environments.

The ultimate objective is defeating the Wither Storm, which permanently alters the world’s difficulty.

Core Design Philosophy

Night is deadly

Caves are worse

Automation over brute force

Progression unlocks safety

Traditional early-game strip mining and night exploration are intentionally punishing. Players must innovate rather than grind.

Gameplay Features
Hostile World Scaling

Increased mob damage

Increased mob health

Higher spawn frequency in caves

Highly dangerous nighttime overworld

Mining Is High Risk

Underground mobs are significantly stronger

Early manual mining is discouraged

Ore gathering is intended to be automated

Tech-Driven Survival

Players are expected to:

Build rotational machinery using Create

Develop powered ore processing and digital storage via Mekanism

Transition from manual survival to industrial dominance
